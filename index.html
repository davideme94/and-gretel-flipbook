About this edition

This flipbook layout was designed to feel like a gilded fairy-tale object: dark emerald tones, warm gold details, framed spreads, and a dramatic page-turn reveal.

The story moves through loss, silence, and survival while reworking the language of fairy tales into something sharper and more historical.

“The world doesn’t change. You just learn to see it the way it is.”
